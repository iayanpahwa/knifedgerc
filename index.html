Welcome to KnifeEDGE RC Aeromodelling Club Delhi/NCR

About: Proudly launching a new community-driven RC Aeromodelling club in Delhi/NCR - KnifeEdge with the motive to promote RC Hobby and STEM learning.

Co-Organizer: This club is an official chapter of MultiGP drone racing community. For FPV Drone/MiniQuads Pilots, Please register yourself as MultiGP Pilot at https://multigp.com and add this chapter to your timeline.

Location: Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida Expressway with ample green flying area and parking space.
